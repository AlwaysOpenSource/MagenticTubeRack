ASSEMBLY INSTRUCTIONS
	1) Print all parts and verify fit. Some cleaning may be necessary to get tubes and magnets to fit.
	2) Place 2 magnets on each side of the holes in the middle plate.  Making 4 magnets per hole.
			The plate is designed so the magnets will hold themselves in the hole securely. However, for long term
			use and storage, it is reccomended to glue the magnets in place.
	3) Place a small amount of glue along the long-axis edges of the middle plate
	4) Making sure the magnets are CLOSER TO THE TOP PLATE, place the middle plate in the joints of the top and bottom plates
	5) Before the glue sets, ensure all edges are aligned and the plates fit snugly together.
	6) Follow your glue's instructions for drying time.
